Membership Options

Find the Right Membership for You

There's a place here for everyone — whether you're a competitive bowler, someone who enjoys a social game, or simply looking for a friendly club to be part of. Fees are paid in person at the club. All new members are warmly welcomed.

Most Popular
Full Playing
£75 / year (£15 for U21)

For those who want to get on the green — outdoors in summer, short mat in winter.

  • Full outdoor & short mat bowling
  • League and competition fixtures
  • Access to all social events
  • Bar membership included
  • Use of coaching facilities
Social
£10 / year

Love the atmosphere but not necessarily the bowling? Social membership is for you.

  • Access to club bar & social area
  • All social events and quiz nights
  • Non-playing participation welcome
Junior
Free

For younger players who want to learn the game in a welcoming environment.

  • Full outdoor & short mat bowling
  • Dedicated coaching support
  • Junior competition fixtures
Paying your fees: Membership fees are paid in person at the club. If you have any questions about membership before applying, please get in touch.
