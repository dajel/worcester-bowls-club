About the Club

A Hidden Gem in the Heart of Worcester

Tucked away in a quiet corner of Worcester city centre, our club has occupied the same peaceful spot since 1867. Whether you've never picked up a bowl in your life or you've been playing for decades, you'll find your place here.

Our two qualified coaches are on hand to take complete beginners from their very first roll right through to competitive play — at whatever pace suits you. And when the game's done, the bar and social area are waiting.

During the summer we run friendly fixtures across the county, plus league matches in the Worcester and Malvern leagues. Come winter, short mat bowling keeps the arms in shape, alongside poker nights, quiz evenings and other social events throughout the year.
